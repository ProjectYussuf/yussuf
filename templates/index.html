    How to Play Yussuf

    • Objective: Finish each round with the lowest total card value. Last player not eliminated wins.
    • Card values:
      Red King (♥♦) = −1
      Joker = 0
      Ace = 1
      2 through 9 = face value
      10, Jack, Queen, Black King (♠♣) = 10
    • Setup: Each player gets 4 cards in a 2×2 grid. You may peek at your bottom 2 cards once before play begins.
    • Your turn: Draw from the face-down deck or the face-up discard pile, then either replace one of your cards or discard what you drew.
    • Call Yussuf: On your turn, if your true total is 5 or lower, call Yussuf instead of drawing. Every other player gets one final turn. If your total is strictly the lowest, you get −5 bonus. If not, you get +10 penalty.
    • Matching-discard: Any time a card lands on the discard pile, you may try to discard a card of the same rank but different suit. Correct = removed from your board. Wrong = you receive a punishment card. If you match an opponent's card correctly, they get it back plus a punishment card.
    • Special cards (deck draws only): 7/8 = look at one of your own cards · 9/10 = look at one opponent card · Jack = swap any 2 cards · Queen = look at 2 cards then swap any 2.
    • Scoring: Each round your board value is added to your score. Reach exactly 50 → score halved to 25. Exceed 50 → eliminated.
